Caprese Sandwich Recipe

Description

Amazing italian sandwich with tomato basil and mozzarella!

Ingredients

  • 1 head garlic
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 loaf ciabatta bread
  • 1 tablespoon dried rosemary
  • 10 leaves fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, sliced
  • 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Cut head of garlic and drizzle the clobes with olive oil and nestle in aluminum foil. Place in preheated oven for 35 minutes and remove from oven, leaving oven on. Remove garlic from cloves and mash into paste.
  3. Spread butter on cut sides of bread, sprinkle with rosemary, and place on a baking sheet. Toast bread in preheated oven for 5 minutes. Remove from oven and spread with garlic paste. Top one half of loaf with basil and tomatoes. Drizzle tomato slices with balsamic vinegar. Top remaining half of loaf with sliced mozzarella. Return sandwich halves to baking sheet.
  4. Place in hot oven and cook until the cheese has melted, about 10 minutes. Allow sandwich to cool slightly before assembling. Cut into 4 pieces to serve.
